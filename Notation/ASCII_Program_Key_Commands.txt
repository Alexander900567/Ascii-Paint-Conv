Cheat Sheet:

Choose Letter: i + Letter/Key
Copy to Clipboard: b
Undo: z
Redo: y

Regular Mode: cf
Line Tool: cl
Rectangle Tool: cr
Filled Rectangle Tool: cs
Q1`3 Circle Tool: co

Text Mode: ct (Exit with Escape)
Picture Mode: cp
